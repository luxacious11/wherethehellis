Adder
Vipera berus
cold and calculating // they’ll know when they’ve pushed you too far // eyeliner sharp enough to cut glass // neon lights obscured by smoke // dozens of voicemails you haven’t listened to
Crow
Corvus brachyrhynchos
creative and untethered // collecting trinkets from all the places you’ve been // a crowded corner booth at a bar // wholeheartedly committing to the bit // always on the move, can’t sit still for long
Deer
Odocoileus virginianus
anxious and mutable // always looking to the past or the future, never the present // the sun shining through rain clouds // changing your mind at the last minute // following the leader
Eagle
Haliaeetus leucocephalus
noble and proud // always having exactly the right words to say // spending an hour in front of the mirror before walking out the door // putting your best foot forward // chasing adrenalin just to feel anything at all
Otter
Lontra canadensis
loyal and unapologetic // swearing in front of your grandparents // bruises you don’t remember getting // dancing in the middle of a rainstorm // the family you choose is the family that matters
Rabbit
Oryctolagus cuniculus
playful and cunning // always on alert, even when you don’t look it // bodies covered in elaborate decorations // smiles that don’t always reach your eyes // finding comfort in the presence of others
Tiger
Panthera tigris
thoughtful and intuitive // rain so loud it drowns out the sound of traffic // a life of solitude, but not by choice // feeling every emotion with your entire being // ink smeared on a weathered page
Wolf
Canis lupus
courageous and faithful // clouds covering the moon at night // bruises concealed beneath make-up // willing to die for the ones you love // singing when nobody’s around to hear you
